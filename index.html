About

I'm Sam Richman, a writer, filmmaker, and line drawing (pictured above).

My directing work focuses mainly on narrative fiction film, though I have also directed documentary, music video, and theater work. I also freelance as an editor on commercial projects with creative agencies and teams in LA, NYC, and abroad.

I live in Los Angeles, where I cook, craft, and climb. Learn more in my resume or just ask.
